Oculus Quest


Believe me when we say we weren't expecting Facebook's Oculus Quest to be one of our top gadgets of 2019. The VR headset blew me away when it being introduced at CES and then again when i got to watch review of famous youtubers in the tech space play an extensive library of immersive VR games (like Beatsaber and Super Hot) when it launched in the spring. Quest isn't a breakthrough for VR simply because it doesn't require a beefy PC or smartphone or external sensors to track your head and hand movements. It's a new milestone for VR because it's actually fun to play with. The same can't be said for pretty much all other VR headsets. You can snag this for 399 USD
